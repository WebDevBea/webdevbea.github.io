Countertops

Granite

Granite countertops are a popular choice for their durability and heat-resistance, and they come in a range of unique patterns and colors that can add character to a kitchen. However, they are porous and require regular sealing to prevent staining.

Quartz

Quartz countertops are low-maintenance, scratch-resistant, and available in a variety of colors and patterns, making them a popular choice for modern kitchens. They're also non-porous, making them resistant to stains and bacteria.

Laminate

Laminate countertops are a budget-friendly option that are easy to clean and come in a variety of colors and designs, but they can be prone to scratches and chips and may not last as long as other materials.

Marble

Marble countertops offer an elegant and timeless look, with a luxurious finish that can add value to a home, but they require regular maintenance to prevent staining and are prone to scratches and chips.

Concrete

Concrete countertops offer a modern, industrial look and can be customized to fit any kitchen design, but they can be prone to cracking and require regular sealing to prevent staining. They're also extremely heavy and may require additional support.
